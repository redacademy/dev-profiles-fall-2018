Life Before RED

Hometown: I was born in Montreal, but grew up in Vancouver for most of my life.

Before applying to RED, I got a Bachelor of Science from UBC. I originally wanted to study comp sci, but my mom discouraged it at the time. One day, I saw her struggling to turn on a computer, and I had a vision of myself struggling to turn on my grandchildren's hologram. In this rapidly growing field of tech, I knew I did not want to fall behind. Over the years, my passion in this area grew and eventually, it led me to apply for RED.

Best Travel Experience

In February 2018, I embarked on a backpacking trip to Southeast Asia. What was suppose to only be a two month long trip extended to five months. I started in Thailand and after I decided to extend my backpacking trip, I visited Laos, Vietnam, and Indonesia. The trip gave me the opportunities to do things that would have normally been out of my comfort zone, including scuba diving, cliff jumping, a week-long motorbike tour in Vietnam, and I had the opportunity to paint a mural in Bangkok and Pai, Thailand! Both times were "Rick and Morty" themed. I got to meet like-minded travelers from all over the world, experience different types of food and learn new cultures.
